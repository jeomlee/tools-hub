About this tool

JPG to PDF allows you to combine multiple JPG or PNG images into a single PDF file. The conversion runs locally in your browser without uploading your images. This tool is ideal for scanning documents, creating simple reports, or merging photos into one printable PDF.

All processing happens on your device. No files are uploaded.
